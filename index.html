How UK Pharmacy Works

  1. Patients receive prescriptions and visit pharmacies.
  2. Certain groups pay nothing; others pay £9.35 per item.
  3. Government reimburses pharmacies based on quarterly Drug Tariff.
  4. Profitability hinges on efficient dispensing and low-cost purchasing.

Why can’t medicines be sold directly on Amazon?
